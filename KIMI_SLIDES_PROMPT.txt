Create a 16-slide ultra-premium sales presentation for "KodSpot" — a digital QR menu SaaS platform built exclusively for Indian restaurants, cafes, and hotels. This presentation will be shown to a restaurant owner client in a face-to-face meeting.

BRAND IDENTITY & DESIGN SYSTEM:
- Brand name: KodSpot (logo text style: "Kod" in dark navy #0f172a, "Spot" in gold gradient #c68b52 to #e2a86b)
- Primary color: Warm gold #c68b52
- Secondary: Deep navy #0f172a
- Accent: Soft cream #fffbf5
- Text dark: #1e293b
- Text light: #64748b
- Success green: #059669
- Background: Clean white with subtle warm gold radial gradients
- Typography: Modern sans-serif (Inter or similar), bold headings, clean body
- Design mood: Ultra-premium fintech/SaaS aesthetic — think Stripe, Linear, or Razorpay pitch decks. Minimal, lots of whitespace, refined

DESIGN RULES:
- Every slide must have generous whitespace — never crowded
- Use warm gold (#c68b52) as the dominant accent, not blue
- Dark navy (#0f172a) for headers and contrast sections
- Subtle gradient backgrounds (cream to white, or dark navy to slate)
- Use rounded cards with soft shadows for feature callouts
- Icons should be simple line-style or emoji-style, never clipart
- No stock photos of people or generic restaurant images
- Instead of fake screenshots, use abstract representations: colored cards, simple rectangles with rounded corners showing layout hints, or use flat illustration style showing phone outlines with colored blocks inside
- For phone mockups: use a simple flat phone frame (rounded rectangle with notch) and inside show colored blocks and text labels that represent the UI layout — NOT realistic screenshots
- Charts/graphs should use gold (#c68b52) and navy (#0f172a) color scheme
- Every slide should feel like it belongs in a ₹10 crore startup pitch deck

GLOBAL DESIGN RESTRICTIONS (IMPORTANT — DO NOT IGNORE):
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. DO NOT use any real screenshots or fake UI images. Use ONLY abstract flat colored shapes (rectangles, circles) to represent phone screens, dashboards, or menus.
2. DO NOT use stock photos of people, food, or restaurants.
3. DO NOT invent features that are not listed above. Stick to exactly what is described.
4. DO NOT use blue as the primary accent color. The brand color is WARM GOLD (#c68b52). Navy (#0f172a) is for dark backgrounds and headings only.
5. DO NOT make any slide look crowded. Every slide must have at least 25% whitespace.
6. DO NOT use generic clipart. Icons should be clean emoji-style or simple line icons.
7. DO NOT add animations or transitions in the design — this is for a static slide deck.
8. DO NOT use any competitor brand names (Zomato, Swiggy, etc.) anywhere in the slides.
9. DO NOT show any fake user testimonials or made-up statistics. All numbers must be exactly as provided.
10. Keep all text in English. No Hindi or regional language text.
11. The "RECOMMENDED" tag on the Standard plan should be a gold pill badge, NOT red or green.
12. Slide aspect ratio: 16:9 widescreen
13. Every slide must feel cohesive — same color palette, same typography weight hierarchy, same spacing ratios.
14. Phone frame mockups should be simple flat rectangles with rounded corners (radius ~40px for outer border, ~30px for screen area) and a small notch cutout at the top center. Inside the screen, use solid colored rectangles and text labels only — never photorealistic content.

TONE:
- Professional but warm, not corporate-cold
- Confident but not arrogant
- Premium but accessible — this is an affordable product positioned as high-quality
- Speak as a founder who personally cares about the client's restaurant
- Use "your restaurant", "your customers", "your menu" — make it personal
