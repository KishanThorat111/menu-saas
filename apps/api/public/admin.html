⭐ Review QR (Back Side)

Add a Google/Zomato review link — the back side of your QR card will show a "Leave a Review" QR instead of a duplicate menu QR.

How to get your Google review link
  1. Open Google on your phone (not Maps)
  2. Search your business name e.g. "Hotel Sai Palace Pune"
  3. You'll see "Your business on Google" panel
  4. Scroll down and tap "Ask for reviews"
  5. A popup opens — tap "Copy link"

You'll get a link like: https://g.page/r/XXXX/review

Also works with Zomato, Swiggy, TripAdvisor, or any review page URL.

💰 UPI Pay (Pay Now Button)

Let customers pay you directly from the menu. A "Pay Now" button will appear on your digital menu — no physical QR needed at the table.

🔒 UPI Pay is available on Standard and Pro plans. Upgrade now
What is a UPI ID?

Your UPI ID is the address linked to your bank account used for receiving payments via UPI apps (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm, etc.).

It looks like: yourname@ybl or 9876543210@paytm

You can find your UPI ID in any UPI app → Profile / Settings.

⚠️ Payments go directly to your bank account. KodSpot does not process or verify any transactions.
