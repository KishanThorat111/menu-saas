FAQ

Common questions, straight answers

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do my customers need to install an app?
No. Customers scan the QR code with their phone camera. The menu opens directly in their browser — it works on every smartphone without any download.
Can I update my menu after setting it up?
Yes. Log in to your dashboard anytime to add, edit, or remove items, change prices, upload photos, or switch themes. All changes are reflected on your live menu instantly.
What happens after the 14-day free trial?
After you submit the trial request, we personally set up your restaurant's menu and send you login details via WhatsApp. Your 14-day free trial starts from the day you receive your credentials. After the trial, you choose a plan that fits your restaurant. If you decide not to subscribe, your menu enters a grace period before it expires. There are no automatic charges — you pay only when you choose to.
Will my printed QR codes keep working if I update my menu?
Yes. Your QR code and menu URL are permanent. No matter how many times you update items, prices, or themes — the same printed QR code always shows your latest menu.
How do I make payments?
We accept payments via UPI, bank transfer, and secure online payment links. You will receive a reminder before your subscription is due — no surprise charges.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. All plans are month-to-month. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel at any time without penalties or lock-in periods.
What is your refund policy?
We offer a full refund within 7 days of your first paid subscription if you're not satisfied. After 7 days, you can cancel anytime and your menu stays active until the end of your billing cycle. Read the full Refund Policy.
Is my restaurant data safe?
Yes. We use HTTPS encryption, hashed PINs (your PIN is never stored in plain text), and our data practices are aligned with India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act (DPDPA). Your data is stored on secure servers and is never shared with third parties.
